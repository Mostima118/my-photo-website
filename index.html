About Me

Hello! I'm Wilson, a passionate photographer with over 10 years of experience behind the lens. My love for photography began with a deep appreciation for the natural world, and to this day, I spend most of my time capturing breathtaking landscapes, wildlife, and serene moments in nature. Whether it’s the quiet beauty of a forest or the subtle movement of animals in their habitat, I strive to preserve the emotion and atmosphere of every scene I witness.

In the last few years, my interests have expanded to include aviation and automotive photography — fields that combine my love for speed, precision, and engineering. I find joy in capturing the power and elegance of aircraft in flight and the sleek lines of vehicles in motion.

Looking ahead, I'm excited to grow as a photographer by exploring portrait photography. I’m eager to connect with people and tell their stories through light, composition, and emotion. No matter the subject, my goal is always the same: to capture authenticity and share the beauty I see with the world.
